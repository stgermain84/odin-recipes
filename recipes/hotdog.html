Chicago-style Hot Dog

Description

This is a hot dog from the Windy City

Ingredients
  • 1 all-beef hot dog
  • 1 poppy seed hot dog bun
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon sweet green pickle relish
  • 1 tablesppon chopped onion
  • 4 tomato wedges
  • 1 dill pickle spear
  • 2 sport peppers
  • 1 dash celery salt
Steps
  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil over high heat.
  2. Place a streamer basket into the post and steam poppy seed bun until warm.
  3. Place hot dog in steamed bun. Pile on topping in this order: mustard, relish, onion, tomato, pickle, peppers, and celery salt.
